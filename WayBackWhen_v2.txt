**********LEGEND**********

.        palm mute
x        left hand mute
จ        let previous note play for a while before next action (which is usually a slide, bend, pull-off etc)
15b17    bend up one step (to a note that's equal to fretted 17) from fret number 15. Often these are "instant".
pb17r15  prebend up one step at fret 15 and release back to fretted 15 
d        downstroke (only tabbed if there's a need to emphasize this)
u        upstroke (only tabbed if there's a need to emphasize this)
h        hammer-on
p        pull-off
~        vibrato
/ or \   slides: 12/13 is instant slide to 13. 12จ/13 you first linger on 12 for a while.
S        silence / mute string / killswitch
[7]      natural harmonic on top of fret 7
AH       artificial harmonic
lrจจจจจ  let strings ring as long as indicated
(7)      ghost note = just played somewhat quieter or it's just a very brief note. Make figuring out the accents easier.
